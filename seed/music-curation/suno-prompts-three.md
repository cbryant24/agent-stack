## Key Learnings

### What Works for This Sound

- **"Solo synthesizer only"** — explicit solo framing is essential; Suno defaults to adding accompaniment
- **"No drums, no bass, no percussion"** — must state absences explicitly or Suno fills the space
- **Reverb language matters** — "deep cosmic reverb", "reverb trails", "cosmic echo depth" all push toward spaciousness
- **Phaser modulation** is a reliable keyword for the psychedelic space quality
- **"Vast empty space between notes"** encourages Suno to breathe and not overfill
- **Rhodes / electric piano** produces a warmer, more organic keyboard tone
- **"Analog synthesizer"** produces a more synth-forward, oscillator-driven sound
- Combining both ("electric piano synthesizer") blurs the line usefully

### What Didn't Work

- **Faster tempos (92–100 BPM)** with edge keywords ("squelching", "acid", "aggressive resonance") pulled Suno away from the space psychedelic sound entirely — ended up too mechanical and industrial
- **Too many instruments** in early prompts diluted the keyboard/synth focus
- **"Motorik rhythm"** introduces a driving beat that competes with the floating quality

### Suno Behavior Notes

- Suno tends to sneak in rhythm elements even when mood keywords suggest stillness — explicit negation ("no drums", "no percussion", "no rhythm section") is necessary
- If Suno still adds percussion after explicit negation, add `[Instrumental]` as a lyrics marker and regenerate
- Tempo range **60–75 BPM** is the sweet spot for this style — slow enough to float, not so slow it stalls
- "Unaccompanied" and "isolated keyboard voice" are strong framing words for solo output

### Prompt Construction Pattern for This Style

```
[Genre]: Space psychedelic / Psychedelic space ambient
[BPM]: 60–75
[Solo declaration]: solo synthesizer only / unaccompanied analog synthesizer
[Negations]: no drums, no bass, no percussion, no rhythm section
[Reverb/space]: deep cosmic reverb, reverb trails, vast interstellar space
[Modulation]: phaser drift, LFO modulation, filter movement
[Mood]: hypnotic, meditative, contemplative, weightless, ethereal
[Space keywords]: interstellar, cosmic, interplanetary vastness
```
